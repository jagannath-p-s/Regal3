Regal Industries started its humble journey with a commitment to delivering high-quality steel products. Established in 2006 by Mr. Ravindran in Changaramkulam, Kerala.
In 2014, the company embarked on a significant transformation, relocating to Kappur in the Palakkad district. This move marked a new phase of expansion and ambition, further highlighted by an inauguration event attended by MLA VT Balram.
Today, under the Directorship of Vishnu Ravindran, Regal Steel Industries offers an extensive range of over 100 products, specializing in powder-coated items known for their durability and quality. The company provides a variety of furniture for auditoriums, banks, and educational institutions, fulfilling bulk orders throughout Kerala.
